# Admin Guide

For school owners, principals, and administrators.

## Logging In
Use credentials from registration or accept an email invitation.

## Onboarding Staff
1. Navigate to /(admin)/invitations
2. Click "+ Invite"
3. Enter email + select role (16 options)
4. Staff receive email with accept link

## Managing Branding
/(admin)/settings — logo_url, primary_color, accent_color, motto

## Managing Subscription
/(admin)/subscription — view plan, usage, upgrade

## Custom Domains
/(admin)/domains — add custom domain, configure DNS TXT, verify

## Student Admission
/(admin)/students — admit, transfer, graduate, suspend

## Finance Overview
/(admin)/finance → /(bursar)/ for detailed management

## HR Management
/(admin)/hr — staff, payroll, leave, performance

## Academic Management
/(admin)/academic — academic years, terms, subjects, classes, streams

## Analytics
/(admin)/analytics — school overview, finance, attendance, AI usage

## Audit Logs
/(ict)/logs — recent activity. /(superadmin)/audit for platform-wide.
